Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 18, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: Telugu Quran App is committed to protecting your privacy. We do NOT collect any personal information directly. However, this app displays advertisements that may collect certain anonymous data for ad personalization. Read below for complete details.

1. Overview

Telugu Quran ("we," "our," or "the App") is a free mobile application that provides access to the Holy Quran in Telugu script. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in our application.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect Directly

We want to be clear about what we do not collect ourselves:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information
  • Precise Location: We do not track your exact GPS location
  • Contacts: We do not access your contacts or phonebook
  • Photos/Media: We do not access your photos, videos, or media files
  • Camera/Microphone: We do not use your camera or microphone

2.2 Information Collected by Ad Partners

Our advertising partners (Google AdMob) may automatically collect:

  • Advertising ID: Anonymous identifier for ad targeting (can be reset in device settings)
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, screen size
  • Approximate Location: Country, region, or city level (for regional ads)
  • IP Address: For ad delivery, fraud detection, and approximate location
  • Ad Interaction Data: Which ads you view or click on
  • App Usage: How you interact with the app (for analytics and ad optimization)

Important: This data is collected by our advertising partners, not by us. We do not have access to or control over this data. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores certain data only on your device to provide functionality. This data never leaves your device:

3.1 Reading Preferences

  • Last Reading Position: The Surah and page number where you last stopped reading

    6. Children's Privacy

    Our app is suitable for all ages, including children. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by:

    • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, emails, or personal information from anyone, including children
    • No Registration Required: No account creation needed
    • Age-Appropriate Ads: Only non-personalized, family-friendly ads are shown to children under 13
    • Parental Control: Parents can disable ad personalization in device settings
    • No Social Features: No chat, messaging, or social media integration

    For Parents: This app shows advertisements that may be powered by Google AdMob. These ads are filtered to be age-appropriate and do not collect personal information from children. You can control ad settings through your device's privacy settings.

    7. Data Security

    Since we don't collect personal data, there's minimal security risk. However:

    • Data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's security features
    • Quran content is delivered over secure HTTPS connections
    • Firebase Firestore has read-only public access for Quran content only
    • We regularly review and update our security practices

    8. Your Rights and Control

    You have complete control over the app:

    • Delete All Data: Simply uninstall the app to remove all local data
    • Clear Bookmarks: Remove individual bookmarks from the Bookmarks screen
    • Offline Use: Once content is downloaded, you can use the app in airplane mode
    • No Account Required: No login, no account, no tracking
